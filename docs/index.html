Who We Are

We are twin brothers passionate about coding and design.

And we want to use that passion to help you create an awesome online presence. We'll work closely with you to make your digital vision come to life. We'll use our creativity and technical expertise to help you build an incredible user experience— whether you are looking to create your first site or modernize with a new, fresh look.

Scott Benson

Scott Benson is a web developer with a strong background in design and more than five years of experience in both fields. He currently works for Andrew Davidson and Co. Scott is the co-founder and CEO of education startup Study Clash, as well as the CTO of nonprofit Generation Vote. Scott has also worked as a web developer/designer for two design agencies, Langton Creative and Idea Kraft, as well as a software developer for healthcare technology company Cipherhealth. He graduated from Binghamton University in May 2018.

Bryan Benson

Bryan Benson is a full-stack software engineer with more than six years of experience. He currently works as a software engineer at Amazon. Bryan also works as a freelance web developer. He has worked as a software engineer for Law360, a LexisNexis company, as well as two Manhattan healthcare technology companies, Medidata Solutions and Cipherhealth. Bryan is the co-founder, chief engineer, and CTO of Study Clash, Inc., and graduated with a Bachelor of Science with distinction for computer science from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in May 2018.

What We Do

We have everything you need to enhance your digital presence.

Web Design and Development

We create websites fully tailored to your needs. We make fast, intuitive, and elegant sites that enhance user experiences. Let's work together to build your dream website.

Mobile Friendly Development

Having a mobile-friendly website is vital in today's business environment. Our websites are responsive and look great on any device, giving visitors an optimal browsing experience, anytime and anywhere.

Search Engine Optimization

We adhere to Search Engine Optimization (SEO) principles, allowing our clients to increase visibility in organic (non-paid) search engine results. That means more traffic to your site— for free.

Hosting and Maintenance

We will find the best hosting solution for your website. And our work doesn't end once we've launched your site. We will work with you to mantain your site and make any changes needed after the initial launch.

Branding and Illustration

We like being creative too. Whether you need a new logo or want some graphics to help give your website a new look, we'll help make you stand out. Your brand represents who you are to the world. It's time to reintroduce yourself.

Customer Service

We're a small team, so we will personally see to your needs. We'll work directly with you every step of the way. Any questions? We'll always just be an email or phone call away.
